Our workshops

Two courses, one goal: a better cup

Hands-on sessions in small groups (max. 4 people), machine time from start to finish. Every participant leaves with their extraction recipes and a bag of specialty coffee.

N°1

Mastering Espresso

From bean to the perfect shot

Grind, dose, distribution, tamping and extraction: learn to dial in an espresso from scratch on the Victoria Arduino Prima Pro. Reading the cup, adjusting recipes, milk texturing and latte art basics.

  • Dialing in grind and dose
  • Extraction recipes (ratio, time, yield)
  • Diagnosing under- and over-extraction
  • Milk texturing and first patterns

N°2

Water, Espresso & Filter Coffee

98% of your cup, finally under control

Water makes or breaks a coffee. Minerality, hardness, alkalinity: learn to build your own brew water and measure its real impact, on espresso and filter alike (V60, batch brew), backed by comparative tastings.

  • Water chemistry: TDS, hardness, alkalinity
  • Building your own brew water
  • Filter methods: V60 and batch brew
  • Comparative espresso / filter tasting

Frequently asked

FAQ

Where do the courses take place?

In Beaumont (Rue Orger Meurice 12, 6500 Beaumont), about 30 minutes from Mons, Charleroi and NATO SHAPE. Easy parking nearby.

How do I book a seat?

Use the contact form below or reach out directly on WhatsApp. We confirm the date within 24 hours and send you the practical details.

Do I need experience or equipment?

No experience required: courses suit enthusiasts and future professionals alike. All equipment is provided, coffee included. Courses are given in French, English or Dutch.

Do you offer gift vouchers?

Yes. A barista course makes an ideal gift — contact us and we'll prepare a personalised voucher.
